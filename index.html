About Our School

Brief Description

We don’t just give pupils an education and experiences that set them up for success in life. We help them succeed in life by helping them discover passions and dare them to pursue them.

Vision

We seek to foster life among learners who are agents of their learning and wellbeing; who seek a deeper understanding of, and connection to themselves, others in the school, Uganda, and global communities.

Mission

We are a diverse community of learners who came together to realise our individual and collective learning potential by creating a culture of belonging, opportunity, and challenge.

Our Academics

Baby class

An early childhood educational level (often for children aged 3–4) or a specialized development session for infants

Top class

Top Class is the final year of nursery or pre-primary education before a child joins Primary One.

Primary one

Primary One (P1) is the first year of lower primary school and marks a major transition from the play-based nursery section to formal, structured education.
